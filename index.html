RPC Formula — Core Bid Method
Bid = (Revenue ÷ Clicks) × Target ACOS
Revenue = total 7d sales from that keyword
Clicks = total clicks for that keyword
Target ACOS = your threshold (default 35%)

Example: $500 revenue, 200 clicks, 35% ACOS
→ Bid = ($500 ÷ 200) × 0.35 = $0.875

Superior to flat ACOS tiers because it uses your real CVR and AOV.
